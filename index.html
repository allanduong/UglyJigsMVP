About Ugly Jigs:

A small business run by a group of friends.

Based in Seattle, we locally hand-make each and every one of our squid jigs. Our philosophy is to sell high quality, long-lasting jigs at a reasonable price-point. We are continually innovating and constantly improving our product to best serve you.

"As an engineer, I'm always trying to improve my craft. Over the past few months, my friends and I have been experimenting and testing the best way to make jigs. This started out as a side hobby. But as more and more people have been seeing us catch limit on the pier and asking for our help, we have finally decided to put our product on the market. These jigs are guaranteed to last long and help you catch limit."

Tony Hua-Wong - Founder/CEO
